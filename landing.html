About the App

Welcome to our Weather App — your ultimate source for real-time weather updates! This app is built to provide accurate and reliable weather information, ensuring you're always prepared for the day ahead. Our intuitive design and visually appealing interface make it easy to check current conditions, including temperature, humidity, and more.
